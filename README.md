# VillageRefuge

Place a beacon of tier 1 or > near a bell. <br>
When the village bell is rung, there is a chance a village 'refugee' will appear at the beacon. <br>
The chance of villager spawn is related to beacon tier. <br>

Spawn rates:
<br><br>
Tier 0: 0% <br>
Tier 1: 1% <br>
Tier 2: 2% <br>
Tier 3: 3% <br>
Tier 4: 5%
